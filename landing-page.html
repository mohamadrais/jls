Steps of Getting Hired

1. Register to Our Portal

Give us your basic information which we required such as passport image, profile photo and personal information.

2. We Verified

All your informations will be analyze and verified to ensure there is no fraud attempt.

3. We Approve Your Profile

After your personal information have been verified, we will release your profile to be viewed by the employer.

4. Job Match

Our agent will do the matching between you and any employer who interested to hiring you.

5. Get Contacted by Employer

Employer will then will contact you to conduct an interview session with them.

6. Get Hired!

If everything went well, you might get hired and pursue your dream job!.
